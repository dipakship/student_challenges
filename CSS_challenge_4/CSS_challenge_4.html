Cheese

Cheese is tasty. It's a delicious snack. It's is made with various types of milk, depending on what part of the world you live in. There are also dairy-free cheeses.

Cheese is typically made through the process of curding milk. Today, this is done in factories!
First, the milk is pouring into big containers called vats and then bacteria is added to the milk to convert lactose into lactic acid.
Then, an enzyme called rennet is added to "naturally" curdle the milk.
In the past, rennet was obtained from the stomach of young cows, however now genetically made bacteria and yeast is primarily used to make the enzyme.
